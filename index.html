My Journey

Hello, This is Mari Sankar and I am currently pursuing a B.Tech in Computer Science and Business Systems from Rajalakshmi Engineering College. I have a strong passion for both front-end development and RPA development, and have gained significant experience in both areas.


In Front-End Development, I have experience in creating dynamic and interactive user interfaces using HTML, CSS, JavaScript. In RPA development, I have experience using UiPath to automate tasks, such as data entry and report generation.


Apart from my technical skills, I am a quick learner and work well in a team environment. I am always looking for opportunities to improve my skills and learn new technologies. In my free time, I enjoy playing carrom board and volunteering with local organizations.
